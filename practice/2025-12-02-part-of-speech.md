
#### **Practice Set 1 — 품사 기초 문제 (명사/형용사/부사/동사)**
#### **Q1.**

We need ___ information to complete the report.

(A) additional -> O

(B) addition (명사)

(C) additionally (부사)

(D) additioned

### **Q2.**

The company will ___ customers to any schedule changes.

(A) inform -> O (will + 동사의 원형이 들어와야 함)

(B) information

(C) informed

(D) informing -> X

### **Q3.**

She was ___ to hear the final results.

(A) delight

(B) delighted -> O

(C) delightful

(D) delighting

### **Q4.**

The meeting was delayed because the manager arrived  ___

(A) lately -> X ("최근에" 시간 개념이므로 현재 완료 전용이다)

(B) late (늦은것이기 때문에 부사 late를 사용)

(C) latest

(D) later


### **Q5.**

The editor __ well-known in the publishing industry.

(A) be

(B) is -> O (단수 -> be 동사 is를 사용)

(C) are

(D) being

### **Q6.**

They ___ the customers to improve their service.

(A) survey

(B) surveyed -> O (조사했다이므로 과거형 사용)

(C) surveys

(D) surveying

### **Q7.**

The contract was __ last week.

(A) sign

(B) signed -> O (was + p.p)

(C) signing

(D) signer

### **Q8.**

I am studying English __ the cafe ___

(A) in / now -> O

(B) now / in

(C) at / yesterday

(D) on / soon

### **Q9.**

He travels __ for business.

(A) overseas -> O (이건만 부사)

(B) oversea

(C) oversea’s

(D) overseases

### **Q10.**

We have received several emails __

(A) lately -> O (시간적인 부분)

(B) late

(C) later

(D) latest
